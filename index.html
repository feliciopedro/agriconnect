How it works

From farm to buyer — in three connected steps.

Every role on the platform plays a part. Here's how a single order flows from listing to delivery.

Step 1
📋

List your produce

Upload what you've grown — crop type, quantity, photos, price, and harvest date. Works on the web app, or dial *384# from any phone.

Step 2
🤝

Get matched to buyers

Our matching engine shows your listing to nearby buyers who need your crop. You get notified the moment an order is placed.

Step 3
🚐

Coordinate delivery

Request a transport provider. Your produce is picked up and delivered on an optimised route. Track it in real time.

Step 4
💰

Get paid

Payment lands directly to your mobile money account after delivery. Check your earnings anytime on the app or via USSD.

Step 1
🔍

Search available produce

Browse fresh produce near you, sorted by distance and quality. Filter by crop type, price, and quantity. See the freshness countdown before you order.

Step 2
🛒

Place an order or pre-order

Order available produce immediately, or lock in next season's supply with a pre-order deposit before the farmer plants.

Step 3
📍

Track your delivery

See your transport provider moving in real time and get an updated ETA as they complete earlier stops.

Step 4
✅

Verify and review

Scan the QR code on the crate to verify origin and quality. Rate your farmer and transport provider after delivery.

🗺️

See nearby jobs

Available delivery requests near you appear automatically. Grouped routes let you handle multiple pickups in a single trip.

Step 2
✅

Accept a route

Accept a delivery or a grouped multi-stop route. An optimised stop sequence is calculated using real road distances.

Step 3
🚦

Navigate and update

Follow the suggested route. Mark each stop as arrived and completed. Farmers and buyers see your progress live.

Step 4
💳

Earn per delivery

Earnings are calculated per km and per kg. Payment goes to your mobile money account on delivery confirmation.

FAQ

Common questions.

Joining AgriConnect is completely free for farmers, buyers, and transport providers. We charge a small platform fee on completed transactions to keep the service running and improving.

We are currently live in the Eastern Region, with active operations around Koforidua, Akropong, Somanya, and Nkawkaw. We are expanding to Ashanti and Greater Accra in Q3 2026.

No. You can list produce, check orders, and manage your account by dialing *920*11# from any mobile phone — no smartphone or mobile data needed. The full USSD menu is available in English, Twi, Ewe, and Hausa.

Payment goes directly to your mobile money number (MTN MoMo, Vodafone Cash, or AirtelTigo) after delivery is confirmed by the buyer. No cash handling needed.

A pre-order lets you publish your planting intention — what you plan to grow, how much, and at what price. Buyers pay a deposit to lock in your produce before you plant. This guarantees you have a buyer before spending money on seeds and fertilizer.

Every batch has a QR code. Scanning it shows you the farm origin, harvest date, growing inputs, AI quality grade, and the full delivery journey — all verified by the platform.

Yes. You can save favourite farmers as preferred suppliers and place recurring weekly orders. The platform will notify you when they have new produce available.

Every delivery is tracked end-to-end. If there is an issue, you can raise a dispute through the app and our support team will review the trace record to resolve it.

Earnings are based on a base fee plus GHS 2.50 per km plus a small surcharge for loads over 200kg. You see the full breakdown before accepting any delivery.

When multiple farmers in the same area have deliveries going to nearby destinations on the same day, the platform groups them into a single optimised route — so you make more money per trip and farmers' produce spends less time in transit.
